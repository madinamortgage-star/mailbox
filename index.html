FAQ

Common Questions

Is this a real street address or a P.O. Box?
It's a real Rockwall, TX street address — not a P.O. Box. You can use it for business cards, your website, Google Business Profile, LLC filings, and bank accounts.
Can I use this for my LLC or business registration?
Yes. You can use this address for Texas LLC filings, business bank accounts, merchant accounts, and most state and federal registrations.
How does mail scanning work?
When mail arrives we notify you by email. You can then request a scan, forward the piece, hold it for pickup, or shred it — all from your online portal.
Can I receive packages?
Yes. We accept packages from UPS, FedEx, USPS, and Amazon. You'll be notified on arrival and can arrange pickup or forwarding.
Is there a contract or minimum commitment?
No contracts, no minimums. All plans are month-to-month and you can cancel at any time from your account portal.
How long does setup take?
Your address is activated immediately after checkout — typically under 5 minutes. You'll receive a confirmation email with your address and account details.
